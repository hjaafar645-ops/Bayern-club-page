About

Bayern München is one of the biggest and strongest clubs in football history. Founded in 1900 in the city of Munich, it is considered the most successful club in Germany. The team is known for its attacking style of play and strong fighting spirit on the pitch. Bayern plays at the famous Allianz Arena and has a massive fan base all around the world.

Legends

Thomas Müller

Born on September 13,1989 .. is a veteran player at FC Bayern Münich,Known for his intelligence on the field,
goal-scoring and playmaking abilities, and versatiliy. Since his early Years,he has been a key part of the team
contributing to over 10 Bundesliga titles and 2 UEFA Champions League victories with Bayern, as well as
helping Germany win the 2014 FIFA World Cup.

Oliver Kahn

Oliver Kahn is a legendary German goalkeeper, known for his strong personality and heroic performance.
He played for Bayern Munich and the German national team, and is the only goalkeeper to have won the Golden Ball at the 2002 World Cup.
He spent 14 years with the Bavarian team, winning 8 German league titles, 6 German Cups, the 2001 Champions League, and the 1996 UEFA Cup.

Franck Ripery

He is a former French footballer, known for his exceptional skills as a winger, and his brilliance with Bayern Munich where he won the Champions League and many domestic titles. He was considered one of the best players of his generation, and he retired from playing in 2022. He was known as the "jewel of French football". He won the Champions League, the German League (Bundesliga), the German Cup, and many more with Bayern Munich.

Arjen Robben

He is a legendary Dutch football player, famous for being a fast and skillful winger, especially with his left foot. He played for major clubs such as Bayern Munich, winning many titles including the Champions League, and was known by the nickname "The Flying Winger". He won the Champions League (2013) with Bayern Münich and scored the winning goal in the final, the Bundesliga title 8 times, and cemented his name as one of the legends of the Bavarian club.

Bastian Schweinsteiger

He is a legendary German footballer, considered one of the most prominent midfielders of his generation. He was famous for his triumphant career with Bayern Munich, and he spent most of his career here, played more than 500 matches, and won the German League (8 times), the German Cup (7 times), the Champions League (2013), the Club World Cup, and the European Super Cup.

Franz Beckenbauer

"Der Kaiser" is a German football legend considered one of the best defenders in history. He was famous for his elegant defensive and libero positions. He led Germany to win the World Cup as a player (1974) and as a coach (1990), becoming one of the few who achieved this feat. He also won the Ballon d'Or twice, led Bayern Munich to local and European dominance, and held prominent administrative positions, most notably organizing the 2006 World Cup.

Karl Heinz Rummenigge

He is a German football legend, a prominent former player for Bayern Munich and the German national team, who won the Ballon d'Or twice, the European Championship, and was a runner-up in the World Cup twice. He also held high-ranking administrative positions such as Chairman of the Board of Directors of Bayern Munich and Chairman of the European Club Association (ECA).

Philipp Lahm

A former captain of Bayern Munich and the German national team, he was known for his defensive flexibility (right and left back) and moral dedication. He led Germany to victory in the 2014 World Cup and retired after a career full of local and international titles. He was known for his strong personality and tactical intelligence. He won several German League (Bundesliga) titles, German Cup and Champions League (the historic treble in 2013).

History

Glance

Bayern Munich was founded on February 27, 1900 by 11 football players led by Franz John, The club experienced its golden era in the mid-1970s under the leadership of its legendary star Franz Beckenbauer, winning the European Cup three times in a row between 1974 and 1976, and the team's brilliance has continued ever since,For today's date. Bayern have won the Champions League 6 times, the last time being in 2020 when they beat Paris Saint-Germain as part of the treble, to became only the second team to achieve this feat twice, in addition to their historic sextuple in the same year. Bayern also won the UEFA Cup once, the European Cup Winners' Cup once, the European Super Cup twice, as well as the Club World Cup twice and the Intercontinental Cup twice.
